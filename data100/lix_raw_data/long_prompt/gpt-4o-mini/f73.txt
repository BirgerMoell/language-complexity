To calculate the LIX readability score for the provided Swedish text, we'll first identify the components needed for the calculation:

1. **Number of words (N)**: Count all the words in the text.
2. **Number of sentences (S)**: Count all the sentences in the text. Sentences typically end with a period, exclamation point, or question mark.
3. **Number of words longer than 6 letters (L)**: Count the words that are longer than 6 letters.

### Plugging values into LIX formula:
Now we can compute the LIX score using the formula:

\[ \text{LIX} = \left(\frac{N}{S}\right) + \left(\frac{L \times 100}{N}\right) \]

- \(N = 104\)
- \(S = 4\)
- \(L = 5\)

\[
\frac{N}{S} = \frac{104}{4} = 26

### Step 2: Calculate second part
\[
\frac{L \times 100}{N} = \frac{5 \times 100}{104} \approx 4.8077
\]

### Step 3: Combine both parts
\[
\text{LIX} = 26 + 4.8077 \approx 30.8077
\]
