### Step 2: LIX Calculation

According to the LIX formula:

\[
\text{LIX} = \left( \frac{\text{number of words}}{\text{number of sentences}} \right) + \left( \frac{\text{number of words longer than 6 letters} \times 100}{\text{number of words}} \right)
\]

- Number of words = 96
- Number of sentences = 6
- Number of words longer than 6 letters = 10

First part (number of words)/(number of sentences):

\[
\frac{96}{6} = 16
\]

Second part (number of words longer than 6 letters multiplied by 100)/(number of words):

\[
\frac{10 \times 100}{96} \approx 10.42
\]

### Final Calculation:

\[
\text{LIX} = 16 + 10.42 \approx 26.42
\]
