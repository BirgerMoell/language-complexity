1. **Count the Total Number of Words**: We'll count how many words are in the text.
2. **Count the Number of Sentences**: We'll determine how many sentences there are.
3. **Count the Number of Words Longer Than 6 Letters**: We'll identify which words are longer than six letters and count them.

"Syftet med arbetet är att med hjälp av en nötningsrigg sålla mellan olika material och ytbeläggningar under EGR-liknande förhållanden samt att undersöka olika faktorers inverkan på slitaget. ### Calculation of LIX

Now we use the LIX formula:

\[
\text{LIX} = \left( \frac{\text{Number of words}}{\text{Number of sentences}} \right) + \left( \frac{\text{Number of words longer than 6 letters} \times 100}{\text{Number of words}} \right)
\]

Plug in the values we have:

- Number of words = 80
- Number of sentences = 3
- Number of words longer than 6 letters = 13

Calculations: 

1. **Calculate the first part**: 
   \[
   \frac{80}{3} \approx 26.67
   \]

2. **Calculate the second part**: 
   \[
   \frac{13 \times 100}{80} = \frac{1300}{80} = 16.25
   \]

3. **Sum the two parts**:
   \[
   26.67 + 16.25 \approx 42.92
   \]

So, the final LIX readability score is approximately 42.92.
