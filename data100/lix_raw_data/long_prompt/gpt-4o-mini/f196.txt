To compute the LIX readability score for the given Swedish text, we follow the formula provided: 

LIX = (number of words) / (number of sentences) + (number of words longer than 6 letters) * 100 / (number of words)

### Step 1: Count the number of words
We will count the total number of words in the text.

Text: 
"Redan mer än 8000 år innan upptäckten så bodde indianerna. Men vid slutet av 1400-talet så bodde det halvnomadiska stammar. De levde på samlande, jordbrud, fiske och främst jakt. Under kolonialstyrelsen: Som sagt så erövrade Portugal Brasilien på 1500-talet men inte förrän år 1530 så började man vissa något intresse för sin koloni."

1. Total number of words = 47.

### Step 2: Count the number of sentences
Count the number of sentences in the text. A sentence typically ends with a period, exclamation mark, or question mark.

1. Total number of sentences = 4.

### Step 3: Count the number of words longer than 6 letters
Now we will identify words in the text that are longer than 6 letters.

Words with more than 6 letters:
- upptäckten (10)
- halvnomadiska (12)
- samlande (8)
- kolonialstyrelsen (18)
- erövrade (8)
- Brasilien (8)
- förrän (7)

Total number of words longer than 6 letters = 7.

### Step 4: Plug the values into the LIX formula
Now we will substitute the values into the formula:

- Number of words = 47
- Number of sentences = 4
- Number of words longer than 6 letters = 7

LIX = (47 / 4) + (7 * 100 / 47)

Calculating each part:
1. (47 / 4) = 11.75
2. (7 * 100 / 47) = 14.89 (approximately)

LIX = 11.75 + 14.89 ≈ 26.64

The LIX readability score for the given text is:
