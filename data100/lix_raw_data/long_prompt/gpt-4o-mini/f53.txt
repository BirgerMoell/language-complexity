### Step 2: Calculate LIX

The formula for LIX is:
\[ \text{LIX} = \left(\frac{W}{S}\right) + \left(\frac{L \times 100}{W}\right) \]

Plugging in our values:
- W = 99
- S = 5
- L = 10

Calculating LIX:
\[ \text{LIX} = \left(\frac{99}{5}\right) + \left(\frac{10 \times 100}{99}\right) \]
\[ \text{LIX} = 19.8 + 10.1 \]
\[ \text{LIX} = 29.9 \]
