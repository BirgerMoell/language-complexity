To compute the LIX readability score for the given Swedish text, we will first need to analyze and count the required components:

\[ LIX = \left(\frac{\text{number of words}}{\text{number of sentences}}\right) + \left(\frac{\text{number of words longer than 6 letters} \times 100}{\text{number of words}}\right) \]
