### Step 2: Apply the LIX Formula

The LIX formula is:
\[
LIX = \frac{(Number\ of\ words)}{(Number\ of\ sentences)} + \frac{(Number\ of\ words\ longer\ than\ 6\ letters) \times 100}{(Number\ of\ words)}
\]

Plugging in the values we found:
- Number of words = 72
- Number of sentences = 4
- Number of words longer than 6 letters = 6

\[
LIX = \frac{72}{4} + \frac{6 \times 100}{72}
\]

Calculating each component:
1. \(\frac{72}{4} = 18\)
2. \(\frac{6 \times 100}{72} = \frac{600}{72} \approx 8.33\)

Now, summing these values:
\[
LIX = 18 + 8.33 \approx 26.33
\]

**LIX=26.33**
