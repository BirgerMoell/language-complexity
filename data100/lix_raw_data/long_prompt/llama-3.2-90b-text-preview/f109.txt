Now, we can calculate the LIX score:

(number of words) / (number of sentences) + ((number of words longer than 6 letters) * 100 / (number of words))

= (74) / (6) + ((11) * 100) / (74)
= 12.33 + (1100 / 74)
= 12.33 + 14.86
= 27.19
